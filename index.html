Accident Analysis in Egypt

Data-Driven Insights for Road Safety

Project Overview

Our project aims to analyze road accident data to identify patterns of injuries, high-risk age groups, and dangerous road segments. By transforming this data into actionable insights, we seek to support decision-making processes that enhance road safety and help reduce accident rates.

Main Problem

Road accidents represent a major public safety challenge, leading to loss of lives, injuries, and increased pressure on emergency services. The lack of clear, data-driven insights makes it difficult to identify high-risk areas and take effective preventive actions.

Project Value

1

The dashboard is a key tool to understand road accidents, helping reduce risks, improve traffic safety, optimize emergency services and guide better planning.

2

It highlights high-risk roads and locations, guiding better planning and safety measures.

3

By analyzing age groups and vehicle types, the dashboard guides actions to improve road safety and traffic flow.

4

By tracking emergency cases and outcomes, the dashboard supports faster response and improved medical readiness.
